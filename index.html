Complete Guide: How to Use AI Prompts Successfully

What is an AI Prompt?

An AI prompt is a specific set of instructions or keywords fed into an artificial intelligence system (like Midjourney, ChatGPT, or Bing Image Creator) to generate highly targeted outputs. Writing the right prompt is key to unlocking photorealistic images, 3D artwork, and highly professional copy.
