4. Dominating SEO and Blogging with AI

Blogging combined with affiliate marketing is a proven long-term passive income strategy. However, Google's algorithms now easily detect low-quality, spammy AI content. To rank on the first page of Google, you need "Programmatic SEO" and "Topical Authority" prompts.

Instead of asking the AI to "write an article," you must guide it. Ask it to generate an LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keyword cluster. Then, ask it to create an outline. Finally, feed it information section-by-section.

Example SEO Prompt: “Act as an expert SEO strategist. Generate a topical map for the niche 'Indoor Hydroponic Gardening'. Group the topics into clusters and provide 10 long-tail, low-competition article titles for each cluster. Include search intent (informational, transactional) for each.”

By leveraging these advanced prompts, you can build a highly authoritative blog that drives organic traffic, generates AdSense revenue, and earns you Amazon affiliate commissions while you sleep.

The Anatomy of a Perfect AI Image Prompt

Whether you are using Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or Flux, the structure of your prompt determines the quality of the image. The best prompt engineers in the world use a specific formula to guarantee breathtaking results. Here is the secret formula we use at PromptKaro:

  • Subject: What is the main focus? (e.g., A futuristic samurai, a luxury sports car, a cup of coffee).
  • Environment/Background: Where is the subject? (e.g., in a cyberpunk alleyway, floating in space, on a wooden table in a sunny cafe).
  • Lighting: Lighting makes or breaks an image. (e.g., Volumetric lighting, cinematic lighting, golden hour, neon glow, Rembrandt lighting).
  • Camera & Angle: (e.g., Shot on 35mm lens, DSLR, macro photography, wide angle shot, bird's eye view).
  • Style/Medium: (e.g., Oil painting, 3D render, Unreal Engine 5, anime style by Studio Ghibli, hyper-realistic photography).
  • Parameters: Midjourney specific codes (e.g., --ar 16:9 for widescreen, --v 6.0 for the latest model, --stylize 250).

What exactly is an AI Prompt?

An AI prompt is a specific text-based command or set of instructions given to an Artificial Intelligence model (like ChatGPT for text, or Midjourney for images). It tells the AI exactly what you want it to generate. The better and more detailed your prompt is, the higher the quality of the AI's output.

Is AI generated art copyright free? Can I sell it?

Generally, artwork generated entirely by AI cannot be copyrighted by a human under current US copyright laws. However, this means you are free to use AI-generated images commercially. You can sell them as stock photos, print them on merchandise, or use them in commercial client projects without paying royalty fees. Always check the specific terms of service of the AI tool (like Midjourney or DALL-E) you are using.
